HEALTHCARE AND IA

Description :

Accessing to the advanced medical information is time consuming. A doctor can spend several hours seeking for a specific information about a given disease. However, the search engine of the online scientific libraries such as Scopus, PubMed do not provide such assistance. This project aims at connecting to such online databases and provide the doctor with desired information with synthetic manner. Moreover, it can be provide a pertinence score for each scientific paper.
